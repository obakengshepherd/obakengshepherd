## 🎯 What I Do, And Why You Should Hire Me

I am a **Systems Engineer and Financial Platform Architect** who specialises in designing and building production-grade backend systems, data pipelines, and AI-integrated platforms, specifically for **Fintech, Insurtech, Banktech, and Wealthtech** environments.

My value to a client or team is threefold:

- 🚀 **Rapid execution** — I ship real systems, not prototypes. Every project in my portfolio runs, is documented, and is deployable with a single command
- 🧠 **Systems thinking** — I design for scale, compliance, and correctness from day one, not as an afterthought
- 🔀 **Transferable depth** — my skills cross engineering, data, compliance, product, and operations. Making me useful far beyond a single lane

**I am open to:** remote contracts, freelance engagements, fractional architecture roles, technical consulting, and full-time remote positions.

## 🧠 Engineering Philosophy

<div align="center">

| 🏗️ Principle | 💬 What It Means in Practice |
|---|---|
| **Build for scale, not just MVPs** | Every system I build accounts for what happens at 10x the current load |
| **Security and compliance are not layers — they are structure** | Auth, audit trails, and regulatory constraints are built in from the schema up |
| **Data is the product** | Systems that cannot report on themselves are not finished |
| **Automate what slows humans down** | If a human does it repeatedly, a system should do it reliably |
| **Document the decisions, not just the code** | Architecture Decision Records, READMEs, and runbooks ship with every system |
